Cookie Policy

Our website, Anthony Collins, uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control their use on our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language or other settings, to make your next visit more convenient.

How we use cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies to collect and analyze information about how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our website and provide a better user experience. The information collected by Google Analytics is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Controlling cookies

You can control or delete cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but doing so may affect the functionality of some websites. For more information about how to manage cookies in your browser, please consult your browser's help documentation.
